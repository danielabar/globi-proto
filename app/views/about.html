Welcome to Globi Explorer

Understanding interactions between multiple organisms in an ecosystem is at the core of studies in ecology and biology in general. Globi Explorer is an interactive tool to visually explore species interactions and food webs. It was developed by a team of students as part of Indiana University's online Information Visualization course. The data comes from Global Biotic Interactions, which provides access to species association data through aggregation of various biotic interaction.

Global Biotic Interactions

The infrastructure and service provided by Global Biotic Interactions aims to aid biologists to focus on gathering new interaction data. By providing easy access to biotic interaction data, the project aims to provide an educational resource to better understand and better preserve our ecosystems.
